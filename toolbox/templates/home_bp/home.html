What is the VEDB?

Formally known as the Visual Experience Database, the VEDB contains 717 session of individuals performing daily tasks. Through the use of a custom-designed headset, a recorded session consists of a world-view video and two eye-view videos, as well as various forms of collected data. This dataset mirrors similar efforts by Meta (among others), who uses their's for product development.
